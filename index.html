About Us

Located in Belgrave, Victoria, Eastern Computer Services is a local computer solutions business with a concience.
We have an extensive knowledge of a wide range of computing issues, and take pride in the relationships we build with our clients.
With our eco-friendly policies are at the heart of our values, we specialize in upcycling and recycling devices that have become slow or obsolete. We strongly believe that there is a use for every machine, and would much rather recondition an existing machine before we sell a new PC.

In addition, we are able to offer almost any computing service at low-cost, and are available for call-out in East Melbourne.
If you are unsure as to which service you might need, don't hesitate to contact us for free, no-obligation advice.
